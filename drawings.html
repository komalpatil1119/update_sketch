Drawings

Indian culture is one of the oldest and most diverse cultures in the world. It encompasses various aspects, including Spirituality, Art and Architecture, Festivals and Traditions. These elements blend together to create a unique and rich cultural heritage that is distinctly Indian.

Temples hold significant cultural, spiritual, and architectural importance. Temples are believed to be a bridge between the material and spiritual worlds, facilitating connection to the divine. In this series, I have illustrated this connection.
